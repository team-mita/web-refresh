Benefits of Membership

Resume booster!

Tackle unique projects that give real-world experience. Add to your portfolio and show potential employers the types of challenges you are capable of handling in a team setting.

Meet your fellow students!

Get to know your peers in the IT programs and extend your friendships beyond the classroom. Broaden your tech knowledge by learning from people in different disciplines. (It also happens to be a nice place to de-stress during mid-terms!)

Connect with people working in the field!

Through MITA's ongoing connections with local businesses and organizations, members can participate in a variety of extracurricular activities such as:

  • Attending informational panels, in which IT professionals share their experiences in the industry and discuss the attributes recruiters are looking for in candidates.
  • Touring local businesses.
  • Participating in mock interviews.
  • Making meaningful connections and expanding their network of contacts.

Discover additional clubs where you can explore your particular interests!

The following clubs and volunteer groups also operate under the MITA umbrella:

The Wolfpack Techies offer an in-house computer repair service to Madison College students, faculty and staff. If you are interested in developing computer repair and customer service skills in a business environment, you can find more information about joining as a volunteer at the Wolfpack Techies homepage.
